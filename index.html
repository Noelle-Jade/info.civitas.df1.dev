Welcome to Centra Civitas!

Ramus Civitas Ultima is the realisation of a server I (DualFighter1) concepted in very late 2023 where there is several districts, each having its own mods that are used by the residents.

This is a private server with no end goal other than to have fun with the friends that are in the server. There will not be any form of questing mod in this pack but one day i will dip my toes into making modpacks.
